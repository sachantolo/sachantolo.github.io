LGB topic

The issue, is that we've allowed the narrative to be hijacked. LGB used to mean something specific. We were a sexual minority fighting for recognition and rights. We won those fights. We earned marriage equality.

But now? Now it's become this catch-all identity politics movement. It's diluted everything we built. The L's and G's and B's have real, material concerns. We've faced real persecution. But this obsession with 'gender identity' has nothing to do with sexual orientation. It's a completely different thing being weaponized under our banner.

Sacha Ntolo
